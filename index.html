❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our storage service

When do I pay?

Payment is made on pickup day. We accept MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, and cash.

Is my stuff safe?

Yes! 24/7 monitored facility with security cameras and guards. Your items are insured.

Can I get items back early?

Yes, just contact us 24 hours in advance. Early retrieval fee may apply.

Do you store during all breaks?

Yes! Christmas, Easter, and mid-semester breaks. We're here year-round.

What is the reference ID?

After booking, you'll receive a unique ID (e.g., KDL-000123). Keep it – you'll use it to quickly retrieve your items.
